Steps to Bake a Cake

Follow These Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Grease and flour the cake pan.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Cream the butter and sugar together
  5. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla extract.
  6. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  8. Bake for 30-35 mins
  9. Let the cake cool before serving.
